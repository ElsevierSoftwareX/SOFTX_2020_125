ABOUT

This is the Project Portal for the REINFORCE programme. It is accessible only to members of the REINFORCE Collaboration and contains:

  • a CALENDAR containing important project deadlines
  • information on previous and upcoming EVENTS and MEETINGS
  • links to DELIVERABLES that have been either finalised or are in progress
  • a document repository containing project RESOURCES, such as workshop presentations; formal agreements, etc.
  • information relating to the WORK PACKAGES
  • CONTACT information for the members of the Collaboration
  • a MAP showing where the different institutions involved in the project are geographically situated
  • a list of useful LINKS
